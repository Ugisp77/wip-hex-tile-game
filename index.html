Structure Guide
Transportation
Road
Roads can be placed on 60° angles and must be connected to structures or other roads.
Ship
Ships must be placed on water with an adjacent road, settlement, or other player owned structure. Ships can move up to 6 tiles each turn.
Port
Ports must be placed adjacent to water and a ship. Roads can be built off of ports.
Production

Must be placed on land with a gap of 3 tiles between settlements, and connected to other player owned structures.

Outpost
On number roll produce the most common resource under the settlement, with the quantity equal to the number of that tile under the settlement.
Farm
On number roll produce the most common, and second most common resource under the settlement, with the quantity equal to the number of those tiles under the settlement.
Trade Town
Trade towns must be built adjacent to water. The resource is determined by the most common resource under the trade town. The trade ratio is determined by the amount of the most common tile under the town. 1–4 of the tile = 3:1, 5–6 tiles = 2:1, all 7 tiles = 1:1.
Factory
Factories must be placed on snow. Produces exactly 1 of the chosen resource each round.
Strategy
Walls
Walls can be placed on 60° angles and prevent construction on top of them, effectively blocking areas. Cannot be used to connect structures.
Mercenary Camp
Mercenary camps must be placed on desert. Constructing a mercenary camp allows the player to steal half of one type of resource from any other player. This action happens once immediately after construction and cannot be repeated for the same mercenary camp. Cannot be placed within 3 tiles of other structures.
Castle
Castles prevent other players from building structures, roads, or claiming ruins inside a 6 tile radius. Structures or roads that existed prior to the castle’s construction remain.
Environment
Ruins
Ruins are distributed randomly across the map. The number of ruins is determined by the map size and the number of players. Construction of other structures cannot be done within 4 tiles of an unclaimed ruin, with the exception of roads. Upon constructing a road adjacent to a ruin, the ruin is claimed by that player, granting resources and allowing that player only to construct structures near the ruin.
Bandit Camp
The bandit camp is located in the largest desert on the map, and surrounded by bandit tents equal to half the number of players rounded down. Structures cannot be placed within 3 tiles of the bandit camp.
Bandit Tent
Bandit tents can be moved when the player rolls a 7. When placed next to a resource producing structure, bandit tents reduce its production by half. If placed next to a trade town, trades cost 2x more resources.
